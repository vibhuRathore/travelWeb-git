Rajasthan , India .

Rajasthan is one of the most beautiful, old and colourful states of India. The land of kings and queens and grand forts and palaces, Rajasthan is a hit tourist spot among Indians as well as international travellers.
The magnificent forts and finely carved temples in Rajasthan are counted among the top architectural monuments in the country. These exotic buildings carry a legacy that speaks volumes about the prosperity and richness of the state. It is a state whose cities are nicknamed after various colours, like Jaipur is the Pink City, Jodhpur is the Blue City and so on.

sikkim , india .

To witness the untouched beauty of nature, Sikkim is the best option to explore. The state of Sikkim is renowned for its animals, waterfalls, rivers, lakes, plants, and mountains. It is the most attractive state of India, which is full of sublime natural beauty, magnificent mountains, beautiful waterfalls, and some fantastic landscapes. The Sikkim Himalayas are rich in biodiversity, Sikkim has 144 species of mammals, 450 to 500 species of orchids, 40 species of oaks, 4000 species of flowering plants, 500 to 600 species of birds.The rare blue sheep, Tibetan mastiff, and red panda are also found here.

Uttrakhand , india .

Uttarakhand is one of the best and most beautiful states in India. It is a northern state of India. there are many ancient temples, forest reserves, national parks, hill stations, and mountain peaks that draw large numbers of tourists. There are 44 nationally protected monuments in the state. It is often referred to as Land of gods ( Devbhoomi ) as the state has the holiest hindu shrines. It is the favourite destination of adventure lovers for mountaineering, hiking, trekking, climbing, skiing, camping, paragliding and rock climbing in India.

Himachal Pradesh, india .

Himachal Pradesh, nestled in the majestic Himalayas, is a land of unparalleled beauty. With its pristine landscapes, snow-capped mountains, and charming valleys, it captivates the hearts of all who visit. The towering peaks of the Himalayas provide a breathtaking backdrop, offering panoramic views that leave one speechless. Majestic mountains like Rohtang Pass, Kinner Kailash, and Triund beckon adventurers and trekkers to explore their mystical trails. The serene lakes, such as Dal Lake and Chamera Lake, reflect the surrounding mountains, creating a picturesque setting.
